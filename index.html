Instructions

Move a shape by Left-Click and Drag.

Rotate a shape by Right-Click while Left-Click Dragging.

Score points by covering an N by N square with polyomino shapes that are:
1) All the same color and
2) Each composed of less than N squares.

This will collapse the shapes in the N by N square into a single polyomino composed of N squares.

Example: Cover a 3x3 square with dominos to spawn a tromino:
⇒ or

Win the game by continuing to merge shapes until you form a hexomino (a polyonimo composed of 6 squares).

Careful: Every time you move or rotate a polyonimo a new monomino or domino is added to the board!
